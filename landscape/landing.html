Tirupati-balaji

It is one of the 8 Swayambhu temples
of Lord Vishnu
Most of the devotees visit the temple atleast
once in a year


Vizag Beach

It is one of the biggest beach
in vizag.
Best tourist place during
summer season


Araku Valley borra caves

The valley is renowned for its
delectable coffee exquisite green
countryside and rich tribal
culture.The best time to
visit Araku Valley is September
October, November, December, January
and February.


srisailam Brahmaramba Mallikarjuna Temple

The temple holds very high religious and
archaeological significance to the Hindus
It is said that those who visit the holy Srisailam
atleast once in their life time would be relieved
of their sins, and they would prosper in their lives


Papikondalu

Papikondalu are well known for its historical
and cultural significance.
One can a have a unique experience of staying
in Kolluru (bamboo huts) along the beautiful
hill range.


Ahobilam caves

Sacred abode of Lord Vishnu on Earth
It is the centre of worship of Narasimha
the lion-headed avatar of Vishnu, to
whom the nine Hindu temples and other shrines
all dedicated.


Vijayawada Bhavani Island

It is build in the middle of the krishna river.
It has a robotic dinosaur park, garden maze,
mirror maze, golf and badminton simulator,
cycling, etc.


Sri-Kalahasti-Temple

The ritual Rahu-Kethu Pooja is an essential
and famous Pooja which is shown at the temple
during the solar and lunar eclipses. The devotee
believe the ceremony will protect them from all
effects of the eclipses (Rahu and Ketu).


Amaravathi-Amareswara Temple

One of the unique features of the Amravati stupa
is that the domical stupa structure is covered with
relief stupa sculptural slabs.
The temple in Amaravati, dedicated to Lord Shiva, is
one of the pancharama kshetras in Andhrapradesh.

Rajahmundry-Iskon Temple

International Society for Krishna Consciousness
(ISKCON) world wide organization, which is a non
profit, social welfare & spiritual organization
engaged in propagating the teachings & values of
the great ancient literature of our Vedic heritage.
